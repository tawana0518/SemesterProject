About Newark Coalition for the Homeless

The Newark Coalition for the Homeless is Newarkâ€™s oldest advocacy and direct service organization helping homeless individuals and families. We believe that affordable housing, sufficient food and the chance to work for a living wage are fundamental rights in a civilized society.

Our Values

MISSION: Since 1995, Newark Coalition for the Homeless has led a coordinated community effort with the goal of ending homelessness in Newark, NJ and the surrounding areas.

VISION: We envision a community in which everyone has a stable home and the resources needed to maintain it.
