Newark Coalition for the Homeless

Find Support

Newark Coalition for the Homeless provides healing support for people coping with homelessness, mental health help, drug/alcohol abuse and getting them back on their feet. We hope that you will find resources here to help you or a loved one deal with, and eventually grow from your circumstances.

Counseling

Newark Coalition for the Homeless offers counseling services with Dr.James Monroe. Counseling sessions takes place the first Monday of every month. Counseling services includes:

  • Mental Health Support
  • Substance Abuse
  • Rehabilitation
  • Guidance and Career
Mental Health Support

Newark Coalition for the Homeless offers Mental Health support groups and counseling. Please visit the event calendar under "Volunteer" for scheduled dates.

Shelters

Newark Coalition for the Homeless works with local homeless shelters to find shelter for those who are in need. Some homeless shelters we work with includes:

  • New Community Harmony House
  • Newark Homeless Shelter
  • Urban Renew Shelter
  • Goodwill Rescue Mission
